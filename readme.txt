Display the statistics of your ethOS mining rig on your website so visitors can follow your mining operation.

== Description ==
So, after building your mining rig, it's up-and-running and is mining coins. Wouldn't it be nice to let your visitors
follow your mining operation?

This plugin allows you to display various statistics on your website like hashrates and temperatures.

== Installation ==

- Copy the contents of ethos-statistics.zip to /wp-content/plugins/ethos-statistics
- Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
- The statistics widget is now available
